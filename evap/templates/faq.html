    These are the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

    Where are the data coming from?

    The different information, imported into the system, are provided by the Studienreferat. After that the lectures, their proxies or the student representatives correct any mistakes. If you just found another error please contact the responsible lecturer or the student representatives.

    Who is able to create new users?

    New users are created by the Student Representatives. Ask them if there is the need for new users.

    How should I configure the evaluation period of my course?

    There are different criteria for the period:

    • It should be at least one week long.
    • It should be after the last lecture.
    • It should be before the written exam or the first oral exam.
    Of course there are courses for which it is not possible to fulfil all criteria. Please contact the student representatives if you are not sure.
